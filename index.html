Discover Arlesey

The best of green country living and easy commutes

Set on the Bedfordshire–Hertfordshire border, Arlesey offers the ideal balance of countryside charm, commuter convenience and everyday practicality. Popular with families and professionals seeking more space outside London, the town combines a relaxed pace of life with excellent transport connections.

Surrounded by open countryside, walking routes and attractive villages, the area offers a strong sense of community alongside convenient local amenities, including Ofsted ‘Outstanding’ rated nursery, primary and sixth-form secondary schools, shops and leisure facilities. Nearby Hitchin and Letchworth Garden City add vibrant cafés, independent restaurants and markets a short drive away.

Local transport

  • 1.6 mi Arlesey station · direct Thameslink to London (35 min)
  • 2.2 mi Letchworth Garden City station
  • 3.4 mi Hitchin station
  • A1(M) easy access to Stevenage, London & Cambridge
  • Bus stops adjacent to London Row
